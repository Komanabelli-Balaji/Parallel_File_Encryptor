Historical Discoveries (2025 Perspective)
Oldest Cities: Jericho is currently recognized as one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
Cleopatra Timeline: Cleopatra lived chronologically closer to the 1969 moon landing than she did to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza.
Whale Bone Tools: Archaeologists in May 2025 announced the discovery of the oldest known whale bone tools ever recorded.
WWII Camouflage: During World War II, Boeing disguised a major factory in Seattle by building a fake suburban neighborhood on its roof to hide it from air attacks.
War of the Worlds: The 1938 radio broadcast did not actually cause mass hysteria; newspaper exaggerations at the time created the myth.
First Email: The first electronic mail message was sent much earlier than many realize, back in 1971.
Zero's Origin: The concept of "zero" as a number was first invented in India, representing a massive leap in mathematical history.
Sea Cow Fossil: Fossils discovered in Qatar in December 2025 revealed a new species of sea cow that lived over 20 million years ago.
Olympic Art: Between 1912 and 1948, "competitive art" was an official Olympic sport with medals awarded for architecture and literature.
TB Origin: Genetic analysis in 2025 suggests that tuberculosis may have been transported to the Americas by seals rather than solely by land animals.
